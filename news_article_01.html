2025 Cafe New Coffee Highlight: Mashed Raspberry Cold Brew with Micro-Oxidized Beans

This trending specialty drink blends sun-ripened raspberry puree with cold-brewed coffee made from micro-oxidized beans (a 2025 cutting-edge process that amplifies natural fruity notes without additives) . It’s low-sugar, high-acid, and delivers a "micro-drunk fruity" vibe—fresh raspberry tang balances the coffee’s smoothness, creating a refreshing yet layered taste . Topped with a dollop of oat milk foam (catering to the 34% surge in plant-based coffee demand ), it’s become a viral hit in boutique cafes, especially among young consumers chasing clean flavors and social-media-worthy drinks.
